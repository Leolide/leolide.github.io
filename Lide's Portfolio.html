Clientelling App

Boosting Sales and Enabling Personalized Customer Experiences

Overview

In 2024 Nov, we launched the version 2.0 of our clienteling app, introducing several powerful features to elevate in-store sales and enhance customer engagement. Among these was the Lookbook feature, which I designed to help sales associates create personalized product collections for clients. The Lookbook integrates seamlessly with the inventory system and offers a visually immersive way to present tailored recommendations, redefining the in-store shopping experience.
